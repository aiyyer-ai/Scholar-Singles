How to Play:
The Inventory is located at the top of the screen. Hover over it for access.
The pause button is also located there.
NEVER click the Browser's back arrow!
Clicking on the bottom of the screen will take you back instead.
(The cursor will become feet)
Right click to Rotate! This might be in 2D or 3D space.


You can ask for a hint at any time if you feel stuck.
When you are ready, Click the bottom of the screen.

You have been hired by our Temp Agency and assigned to an eccentric archeologist and scholar looking for new assistants.
